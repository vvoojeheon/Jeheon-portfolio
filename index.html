About

Researching how cities gain, lose, and redistribute opportunities.

My work focuses on the mechanisms through which urban networks, accessibility, and institutional arrangements shape differentiated trajectories across cities. I am particularly interested in why some cities continue to attract people, functions, and resources while others stagnate under seemingly similar conditions.

Across research and planning projects, I use spatial analysis, behavioral interpretation, and policy-oriented inquiry to examine urban change beyond simple agglomeration logic.
